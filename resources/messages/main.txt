Hello! This bot combines the convenience of *Telegram*, the power of *ChatGPT*, and the fun of *Tinder*.

*Chatbot can*:
1. Generate Tinder profile by description 😎
2. Write interesting and intriguing messages to get to know each other. 😘
3. Correspond on your behalf 😇🔥
4. You can practice communicating with a chatbot. 🥰


*Useful commands and links:*
1. /start - bot main menu
2. /profile - Tinder profile generation 😎
3. /opener - message for dating 🥰
4. /message - correspondence on your behalf 😈
5. /date - correspondence with the stars 🔥
6. /gpt - ask a question to the GPT chat 🧠
